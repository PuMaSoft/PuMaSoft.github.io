Usługi
We provide high quality services and solutions, gaining trust among customers and partners.

Project management

We choose appropriate methods and tools to suit individual customer / project environment. We take the best practices of PM-BOK Guide, promoted by Project Mamagement Institute. While doing Agile projects, we apply Scrum approach.

Business analysis

We do complex business analysis, document existing processes and systems to identify possible improvements.
